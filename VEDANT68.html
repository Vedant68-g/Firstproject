1 Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R:

The 2025 Kawasaki ZX-10R is the flagship sportbike from the Japanese motorcycle manufacturer and it is priced at ₹17.34 lakh (ex-showroom).
It is available in one variant and two colour options and is powered by a 998 cc inline four-cylinder liquid-cooled engine.
This unit is mated to a six-speed gearbox with a bi-directional quickshifter and makes 200 bhp at 13,200 rpm or 210 bhp at 13,200 rpm with the RAM air intake. Torque output is rated for 114.9 Nm at 11,400 rpm.
The bike is built around a cast aluminium twin-spar frame with adjustable Showa suspension units.
The ZX-10R’s braking setup includes dual 330mm front discs and a single 220mm rear disc with dual-channel ABS.
It is also fitted with an Ohlins electronic steering damper.
